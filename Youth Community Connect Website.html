About Us

Youth Community Connect is a community-based organisation providing early intervention, referral, and advocacy support to young people. We work collaboratively with families, schools, and local services to ensure young people receive timely and appropriate assistance.

Our Services

Youth Engagement Programs

Weekly activities designed to build confidence, leadership, and social connection.

Referral and Case Coordination

Direct referral pathways to mental health, alcohol and other drug, housing, and employment services.

Life Skills Workshops

Resume writing, interview skills, budgeting, and wellbeing education.
